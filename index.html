Welcome to My Space

(Who's Sherina Zheng anyway?)


An anomaly kept on the fringes of mainstream society, Sherina defied expectations from her small-town upbringing.

Born and raised in a close-knit community, she resisted the family tradition to marry and settle young. With unshakable resolve, she ventured on a winding journey to the heart of it all — the Big Apple, embracing its chaos, challenges, and limitless possibilities.

"As a member of a younger generation unwilling to be confined by mundane norms, I see myself as an outlier, performing boldly on the life stage. Immune to mediocrity and driven by a deep disdain for gender inequality, I am determined to forge any beanten and unbeaten paths.

My professional passions lie at the crossroads of advancing women in STEM and improving outcomes through innovation. I am committed to addressing the inequalities that persist in women’s rights within the industry, inspired by the enduring legacy of Supreme Justice RBG. With her trailblazing spirit as my guide, I aspire to make meaningful impacts and pave the way for a more equitable future."

About


Sherina began her professional journey this fall as an ISO Boarding Associate at a financial technology firm, officially stepping into her career after graduating college.

Within the company, she collaborates with the Product Team on the development of an innovative online booking system for beauty salons, a platform that seamlessly connects merchants, staff, and customers through rapid, efficient solutions that redefine convenience and accessibility.

But this is just the beginning of Sherina’s journey. With a background in Data Analytics, she is driven to explore new horizons in Web Development and Product Management. The satisfaction of building systems from the ground up inspires her—a reminder that progress often begins with bare bones, and every step forward fuels her passion for crafting impactful solutions.
